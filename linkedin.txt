Stop paying ₹500/seat for HR software. 💸

So I built my own — and made it open source. 🚀

Meet PeopleHub — a complete, self-hosted HRMS for the modern workplace. No SaaS bills. No per-seat costs. No vendor lock-in. Just your MySQL, your server, your data.

Here's what's inside 👇

✦ 6 features I'm most proud of:

🔹 Dual-Portal Architecture
HR admins get full control across 14 modules. Employees see only their own data — enforced by role-based middleware at the edge.

🔹 Cmd + K Global Search
Find any employee, candidate, department or document in a single keystroke. Notion-grade UX with full keyboard navigation.

🔹 One-Click Payslip PDFs
Auto-generated monthly payroll with printable A4 payslips — your company logo, earnings breakdown, net pay. Zero PDF libraries needed.

🔹 Recruitment Kanban
Drag candidates across Applied → Screening → Interview → Offer → Hired. Toggle to table view for full pipeline analytics.

🔹 Smart Leave Engine
Real-time balances per leave type — Casual, Sick, Earned, Maternity, Paternity. Auto-deduct on approval, with progress bars.

🔹 100% Self-Hosted & Secure
Runs on your own MySQL. JWT in httpOnly cookies, bcrypt-hashed passwords. Your data never leaves your server.

➕ Plus 8 more modules:
Dashboard · Attendance · Performance · Documents · Assets · Calendar · Reports · Announcements

🛠️ Tech Stack:
Next.js 14 (App Router) · TypeScript · Tailwind CSS · MySQL 8 · JWT (jose) · bcrypt · Plain SQL, no ORM

✅ Why it matters:
• 100% self-hosted — your data stays on your MySQL
• Zero per-seat costs
• Setup in under 5 minutes
• Fully open source — extend, modify, brand it as your own

Building this taught me a ton about RBAC, real-time HR workflows, payroll computation, and designing for two very different user personas inside the same app.
